REGULATION


#Who may participate?

The competition is open to high school students, undergraduate students, and gradaute students of all nationalities. Participants can be individually or in maximum 3 people group.


#How to submit my work?

1. Fill the register form below. Please fill the information of the representative if you are a team.
2. You will recieve an email with a formal register form. Please fill the form and send it back to finish registration.
3. You are successfully registered once you recieve confirmation email.


#Regulation of the landing page

The landing page is limited within one page.
Please host your website on your own server or publish as github page. We will also need you to provide resource code, as a zip file or on github.


#What will be judeged?

Design 20%, programimg skill 50%, RWD + cross platform 20%
